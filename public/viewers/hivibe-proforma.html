STEP 6 Cumulative Cash Position The low point is the SPV target

How to read this chart

Cumulative Cash Position is the SPV's running bank balance across the deployment period — start at $0, deploy capital, see the trough, and watch operations refill it.

The Y0 dip is when the SPV puts capital to work: prototype builds, the first production run of inventory, the Arena platform integration, and a half-year of operating runway.

Each subsequent year, operating cash flow pays back into the balance. The deepest trough is the Peak Cash Need — the floor of the swimming pool, and the minimum the SPV must raise.

Crossing back above $0 is the break-even moment. Anything above is retained operating profit available for reinvestment, distribution, or runway extension.
